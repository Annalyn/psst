About Personal Success Strategy for Teens

Launched in 2017 as a platform for parents & teens alike who believe in accountability, honesty, hard-work and personal growth. This site is a rapidly growing community of like-minded individuals of all ages who foster positive change in our future generations.

It's all in the name:

  • Personal – each client is treated as an individual with unique circumstances, hurdles, goals and needs.
  • Success – Success comes in a variety of forms and each person’s definition of success is different.
  • Strategies – creating a simple, effective plan that is unique to you
  • Teens – 12 – 24 years of age is a series of transitions; from child to adult, from public school to high school, to post secondary education or employment, from living with parents to leaving home, and a few others. This can be confusing, overwhelming and scary.

Together we will:

  • Understand your Core Values
  • Create your Goals
  • Envision a clear road map to your success

Together we can visualize a future full of possibility and easily imagine your self-worth. We will create awareness of limiting beliefs, instill accountability and reinforce self-esteem. We will celebrate accomplishments, enhance productivity and above all; we will learn to do by doing.
